More than game drives

Experiences that match how you explore

Every SHOVELER SAFARI journey is private and built around you—couples, families, and small groups travelling mid-range to luxury. Classic 4x4 game drives are the backbone; around them we layer the moments that make Tanzania personal: birdsong at first light, a balloon over the Serengeti, a walk with an armed guide, or time with Maasai and Hadzabe hosts.

True to our name, birdwatching is our specialist focus—paired with photography-paced drives and family-friendly pacing when kids are on board.

Signature experience

Birdwatching Focus Safari

Inspired by the Northern Shoveler, we design itineraries for guests who want more than a Big Five checklist. Specialist ornithological guiding, slower stops at wetlands and forest edges, and routes that favour Tarangire, Lake Manyara, and the Ngorongoro Highlands when migratory seasons peak.

  • Migratory flyways & endemic species focus
  • Ideal November–May, strong year-round at Manyara
  • Binoculars welcome—ask us about packing lists
  • Pairs cleanly with photography or family trips

Ways to experience Tanzania

Activities you can weave into any itinerary

Mix and match with our 3-day, 7-day, or fully custom Northern Circuit routes. Tell us what you love— we filter the rest.

Core experience

Private Game Drives

Dawn and late-afternoon drives in your own 4x4 with a pop-up roof—window seats for everyone, flexible stops, and a guide who reads the bush with you rather than racing a fixed checklist.

  • All Northern Circuit parks
  • Up to 6 guests / vehicle
Safari Packages
Serengeti

Hot Air Balloon Safari

Float at sunrise over the Serengeti plains—herds as brushstrokes below, champagne breakfast after landing. We book reputable operators into your itinerary when dates and weather align.

  • Best in dry months
  • Add-on to multi-day safari
Ask about Balloons
On foot

Walking Safaris

Leave the vehicle for tracks, birdsong, and the scale of the bush at walking pace—always with an armed, experienced guide. Intimate, sensory, and ideal for guests who want detail beyond the drive.

  • Selected conservancies & parks
  • Fitness-friendly pacing

Specialist

Photography Safaris

Slow-paced drives timed for golden hour, vehicle seating and mount-friendly setups, and guides who understand light, patience, and giving you clean angles—not rushed sightings.

  • Serengeti · Ngorongoro
  • Custom duration

Community

Cultural Tours

Respectful visits with Maasai and Hadzabe communities—stories, daily life, and hospitality that sit alongside wildlife, not as a rushed photo stop. We work with local partners who host guests with dignity.

  • Maasai · Hadzabe
  • Day or multi-day add-on

Highlands

Hiking Tours

Stretch your legs on day hikes around Mount Meru’s foothills or the Ngorongoro Highlands—cooler air, forest birds, and views that reset the safari rhythm between park days.
