Our Story

Inspired by the Northern Shoveler

Founded in Arusha in June 2022, SHOVELER SAFARI was born from a deep passion for Tanzania's landscapes and wildlife. With four years of crafting private Northern Circuit journeys, we work like the Northern Shoveler—a migratory duck that filters for the very best—digging past standard tourist routes to deliver refined, immersive safari experiences.

  • Private mid-range to luxury safaris
  • Expert English-speaking guides
  • Eco-minded & guest-safety focused

Guest safety: Fully serviced 4x4 Land Cruisers with passenger insurance, wilderness first-aid-trained guides, 24/7 AMREF Flying Doctors coverage, and rigorous pre-departure vehicle checks.

Packages include: park fees, meals and bottled water, professional guide, private pop-up roof Land Cruiser, and booked accommodations. Flights, visas, tips, travel insurance, and personal extras are not included.
